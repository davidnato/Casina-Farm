Our Story

Casina Farms

We're a pioneering social enterprise that integrates sustainable agriculture, coastal ecosystem restoration, and community development to create thriving, resilient coastal communities. By supporting smallholder farmers, preserving biodiversity, promoting fair trade practices, fostering climate-resilient agriculture, and improving access to fresh, locally grown produce, we're cultivating a future where people, planet, and prosperity thrive together.

Casina Farms: Building Resilience on the Kenyan Coast

Our mission is to restore ecosystems through sustainable practices, enhancing food security and economic prosperity for smallholder farmers in coastal Kenya while fostering a flourishing natural environment.

From Seed to Sustenance

Having lived on the Kenyan coast, we intimately understood the challenges we faced. Our families fought tirelessly to survive, working hard to put food on the table. Yet amidst these difficulties, we were blessed with a strong sense of community and a profound connection to the natural landscape.

Our Contribution to Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

SDG 1: No Poverty

We empower smallholder farmers to break the cycle of poverty through sustainable practices and fair trade.

SDG 13: Climate Action

We promote climate-resilient agriculture to combat the effects of climate change.

SDG 14-15: Life Below Water, Life on Land

We restore coastal ecosystems to protect marine and terrestrial biodiversity.

Today, Our Dream is Sprouting into Reality

Today, our dream is sprouting into reality. We're nurturing a movement that's revitalising ecosystems and revitalising lives. By empowering smallholder farmers and coastal communities with sustainable practices, we're witnessing a transformation that's nothing short of miraculous.

Imagine It: A Flourishing Ecosystem

Imagine it: a flourishing ecosystem, where every plant and creature thrives in harmony, from the roots of the mangroves to the depths of the ocean. Where the soil is rich and fertile, the water is pure and abundant, and the coral reefs are vibrant and resilient.
