Our Work

By supporting smallholder farmers, preserving biodiversity, promoting fair trade practices, fostering climate-resilient agriculture, and improving access to fresh, locally grown produce, we're cultivating a future where people, planet, and prosperity thrive together.

Our mission

At Casina Farms, we're on a mission to restore ecosystems through sustainable practices, cultivating food security and economic prosperity for smallholder farmers in coastal Kenya, while fostering a flourishing natural environment.

Our Vision

To lead a global movement where smallholder coastal farming communities thrive, supported by healthy ecosystems, prosperous farmers, vibrant local economies, and sustainable practices that guarantee food security for generations to come.
